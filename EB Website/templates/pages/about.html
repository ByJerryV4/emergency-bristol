What is Emergency Bristol?

Emergency Bristol is a realistic UK‑based emergency services roleplay game, simulating real-world protocols, dynamic scenarios, and immersive experiences.

Key Features

  • 🚨 Active policing, firefighting, and medical response
  • ⚙️ Realistic systems for arrests, traffic stops, emergencies
  • 🌧️ Weather & chaotic events like floods, blackouts, and invasions

Why Play EB?

Because you're looking for more than a game. You want a living city simulation, and we're building it.
